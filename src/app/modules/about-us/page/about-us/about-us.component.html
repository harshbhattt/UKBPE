ABOUT
US
UKB Plastic Energy Pvt Ltd is a leading pyrolysis oil manufacturer in India that was founded with an emphasis on sustainable methods. We are committed to converting waste plastic into useful resources. To contribute to a cleaner and greener future, UKB Plastic Energy uses state-of-the-art pyrolysis technology to transform organic resources into high-quality bio-oil. Our advanced manufacturing facilities have been installed with advanced technology that guarantees the effective and ecologically socially conscious manufacturing of pyrolysis oil. With a focus on quality and innovation, we work to satisfy the increasing demand for environmentally friendly alternatives in the energy industry. UKB Plastic Energy Pvt ltd stands at the forefront of the circular economy, offering a viable solution for waste management while reducing the reliance on traditional fossil fuels.
