OUR
Products
Pyrolysis oil, derived from plastic waste, is a versatile and sustainable fuel with extensive industrial applications. Produced through an advanced thermal decomposition process, it serves as an eco-friendly alternative to traditional fossil fuels like diesel and furnace oil. Its high calorific value and combustion efficiency make it ideal for powering industrial boilers, turbines, and heavy-duty machinery. Additionally, pyrolysis oil is a valuable raw material in the chemical and petrochemical industries, where it can be refined into various useful products such as solvents and synthetic gases.
